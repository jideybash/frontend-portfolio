What makes us Different


We're a global, tech-oriented team.
We create your prints and get them where they have to go.


Greatness World is an on-demand order fulfillment and warehousing service that fulfills and ships products including clothing, accessories, and home & living items for online businesses. It’s free to set up, and doesn’t come with monthly fees or minimum order requirements. We produce only what's ordered, making print-on-demand the more conscious choice over bulk manufacturing. Connect your online store with Greatness, and whenever someone buys an item, the order is automatically sent to us, and we fulfill and ship it to the customer.

SERVICES

What we offer


PRINT-ON-DEMAND

Print-on-demand (or POD) is an order fulfillment method where items are printed as soon as an order is made, often without order minimums.

BULK ORDER DISCOUNT

Design and order large amount of product for your team, event or organization and save up to 30%.

WAREHOUSING & FUFILMENT SERVICES

With Warehousing & Fulfillment, you can store products sold at your ecommerce store at our warehouse, and we’ll fulfill your orders and ship the products directly to your customers under your own brand.



CREATIVE SERVICES

Your store needs designs and visuals. If you're struggling with ideas or resources, we've got several in-house teams of talented professionals who can make your brand shine.

DROP SHIPPING SERVICES

With the drop shipping business model, you can sell products in your online shop through a product supplier. So you as the owner don't have to worry about inventory or product storage, simply ship your orders on-demand from your supplier.

TECHNICAL SUPPORT

Although ecommerce software is getting more user-friendly, it can be a lot of work. Let our specialists take the weight off your shoulders.
